# Translation

French: In the eighteenth chapter, you will be able to contemplate how a devout soul must above all else be perfectly humble and by what means one might accomplish this. Eighty-eight. In the first chapter of the third part, O my dearest daughter, you will be able to clearly contemplate how a devout soul profits and progresses in the perfection of the contemplative life by devoutly meditating upon the passion of our Lord and our Redeemer, Jesus. Ninety-two. In the second chapter, you will be able to contemplate how and by what means a devout soul reaches a perfect compassion from the hard suffering of our Lord, Jesus; study this chapter diligently, O my dearest daughter, for certainly, this is the key and the doorway to all perfection. I do not doubt that if you do your duty according to the teachings it holds, you will easily accomplish all the teachings of this book. One hundred.
